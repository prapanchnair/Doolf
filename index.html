About the project

Doolf is a flash flood prediction platform built to provide clear warnings and actionable insights ahead of extreme rainfall events. It is designed for emergency planners, municipal teams, and anyone who needs a better early-warning system.

  • Real-time alerts based on the latest rainfall and watershed data.
  • Visual risk maps for faster decision making.
  • Scalable architecture that fits local and regional deployments.
